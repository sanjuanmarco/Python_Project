DATASET

The LIAR dataset, which was mentioned in the publication "Liar, Liar Pants on Fire: A New Benchmark Dataset for Fake News Detection," was used in this model. The dataset is made up of 12.8K carefully labeled brief statements from PolitiFact.com, together with a detailed analysis report and links to source papers for each instance. . This dataset may also be used to do fact-checking studies.

DISCLAIMER 

The system is limited to inputs written in English, having other languages on the input may give invalid results. NOTE: The decision in detecting fake news from credible news still depends on the users since results are based on the system's prediction.
