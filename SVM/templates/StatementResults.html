The LIAR dataset, which was mentioned in the publication "Liar, Liar Pants on Fire: A New Benchmark Dataset for Fake News Detection," was used in this model. The dataset is made up of 12.8K carefully labeled brief statements from PolitiFact.com, together with a detailed analysis report and links to source papers for each instance. .
